# 24hp × 1U Intellijel Format Stereo Mixer Panel Template

## General Specifications
- **Width**: 24hp
- **Height**: 1U
- **Format**: Intellijel

## Jack Placement Coordinates (in mm)
1. **Input Jack Left**: (10, 30)
2. **Input Jack Right**: (14, 30)
3. **Output Jack Left**: (10, 70)
4. **Output Jack Right**: (14, 70)

## Potentiometer Placement Coordinates (in mm)
1. **Volume Pot Left**: (10, 50)
2. **Volume Pot Right**: (14, 50)
3. **Pan Pot Left**: (10, 40)
4. **Pan Pot Right**: (14, 40)

## Additional Components
- **LED Indicators**: Place at (12, 10) and (12, 90)
- **Switches**: If used, position them at (10, 20) and (14, 20) for channel controls.
